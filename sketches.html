The Anatomy of a Commission

Every great illustration is a series of choices: what to reveal, what to hide, and what emotion to bring to the surface. Before committing to a final render, I provide two unique sketch concepts. This ensures we find the exact heartbeat of your scene. Explore the drafts below to see how shifting the perspective completely transforms the narrative.

Direction I

Quiet Defiance

A more intimate, guarded composition. The tight framing and the protective positioning of her staff and familiar create a sense of brewing tension. The viewer is pulled directly into her space, forced to confront the smoldering, dangerous magic reflected in her gaze.

Direction II

The Commander's Stance

This direction pulls back to establish Alexandria’s commanding presence within the world. Resting the heavy staff casually on her shoulder transforms her posture from defensive to utterly confident. With her familiar grounding the scene beside her, she looks ready to conquer whatever lies beyond the treeline.
